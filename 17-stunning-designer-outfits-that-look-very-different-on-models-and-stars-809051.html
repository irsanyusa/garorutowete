17 Stunning Designer Outfits That Look Very Different on Models and Stars

August 2024 · 3 minute read

With just one piece of clothing, you can create a lot of very different looks. A certain hairstyle, different accessories, or no accessories at all — these details can make the same getup look very different on you than on someone else. And stars know all about this, as they’ll often wear clothes that were modeled on the runway but style them in their own unique way.

We at Bright Side were curious about what the designers’ ideas were before their clothes were eventually worn by different stars. Sometimes, the original idea turns out to be very different from the real-life execution.
