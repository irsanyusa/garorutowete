November1980 Calendar

Election of Interest

  • Nov 4 Sadaharu Oh, 40, pro-baseball's all-time HR run king with 868, retires
  • Nov 8 Voyager 1 space probe discovers 15th moon of Saturn

Bossy Scores Four

Koch's Confession

9 to 5 and Odd Jobs
