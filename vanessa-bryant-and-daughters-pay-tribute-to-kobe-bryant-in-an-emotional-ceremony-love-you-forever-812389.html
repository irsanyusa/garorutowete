Vanessa Bryant and Daughters Pay Tribute to Kobe Bryant in an Emotional Ceremony: Love You Foreve

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Recently, Vanessa Bryant and her daughters paid homage to Kobe Bryant by leaving their own handprints at the historic TCL Chinese Theatre, ensuring that his memory endures for generations to come. The family came together for this special occasion, deeply touching the hearts of fans who witnessed the event.

AFP/EAST NEWS

In 2011, Bryant became the first athlete to have his handprints and footprints immortalized in cement at the world-renowned TCL Chinese Theatre. The ceremony marked a significant milestone in Bryant’s illustrious career, which was characterized by numerous accolades and achievements. It was also a fitting tribute to his remarkable legacy as an athlete, father, and humanitarian.

The ceremony was a momentous occasion, attended by a large crowd of fans and celebrities. “This is a tremendous honor,” Bryant claimed at the time. “I feel extremely honored to be able to do this.”
And, referring to his fans who came out on that day, he confessed, “To have them standing out there is special in every regard, I never in my wildest dreams thought that this was a possibility.”

AFP/EAST NEWS, AFP/EAST NEWS

Vanessa Bryant, Kobe Bryant’s wife, has recently shared photos of the event, wanting to let all of his fans know the importance of that moment.

Bryant’s 2011 ceremony was also attended by his daughter, Gianna Bryant, who tragically passed with him in the helicopter crash. Along with Gianna, his other daughters, Natalia and Vanessa, were also in attendance at the event.

Despite the fact that 3 years have gone by since his tragic passing, Bryant’s family continues to honor his memory. Recently, Vanessa Bryant and her daughters attended a special ceremony at the iconic Chinese Theatre in Hollywood to pay tribute to the basketball star, where they unveiled his handprints.

With a cracked voice, Natalia Bryant, who is currently 20 years old, shared her love and the memories she treasures of her father. “I’m honored to be here today to recognize the man that we all knew and love my dad, Kobe Bryant,” Bryant began. “Being his daughter is one of the greatest joys of my life, and I hope to continue to embody and cherish the many lessons he has taught me.”

Then, Natalia Bryant agreed that it was a significant choice to display Bryant’s prints in front of the Chinese Theater. “This day symbolizes the impact that he has had on a city that he loved and cared for so deeply,” she said. “I encourage you to place your hands in his and take a moment to stand in his shoes. This will be a treasure for the city of Los Angeles and my dad’s fans from all over the world to hold and to cherish.”

As shown in the shared images, there were many emotional moments during the ceremony, and the photos capture the poignant scene of Kobe and Vanessa Bryant’s youngest daughters taking the moment in with wide-eyed curiosity.

Meanwhile, Natalia and Vanessa Bryant were visibly moved, their emotions spilling over at various points during the ceremony. The little girls found solace in placing their tiny hands and feet in their beloved father’s prints, eliciting a tender moment that was watched over by their mother and older sister with heartfelt affection.

The athlete’s legacy shines through Vanessa Bryant’s unwavering pride in her daughters.

Vanessa Bryant took to Instagram to express her admiration for her daughter Natalia Bryant’s bravery in speaking at the event. With pride and love in her heart, the widow acknowledged the difficulty of the moment and celebrated her daughter’s unwavering strength. Her words not only honored her daughter’s courage but also highlighted the unbreakable bond of their family. The post was a testament to the resilience and love that the Bryant family shares, even in the face of hardship.

Natalia Bryant wrapped up her speech with an ultimate tribute to her “daddy,” making sure to also mention her late sister, Gianna Bryant. She concluded, “Dad, you’re an icon, a legend, the storyteller, and most importantly, the best girl dad any young woman could ever dream of,” she said.

“I’m honored to be here to represent you and our family, and we are extremely proud of you. We will love you forever and always. Love, Natalia, Gianna, Bianka, Capri, and Mommy.”
