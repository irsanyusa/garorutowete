Regina Daniels Shows Concern About Protest, Urges Nigerians to be Peaceful: &quot;Madam Stand Up &amp; Join&quot;

June 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Regina Daniels, the wife of Nigerian senator Ned Nwoko, has shared a video where she addressed Nigerians about the protest
  • It is no longer news that the nationwide protest began on Thursday, August 1, 2024, and will run till the 10th day of the month
  • Regina urged Nigerians to be peaceful while protesting, noting that violence defeats the main purpose

Nigerian actress and politician Regina Daniels has shared a post concerning the nationwide protest against hunger and bad governance.

The mother-of-two acknowledged that she understands Nigerians' state of mind and their thirst for a better nation.

The actress stated that violence will only defeat the sole purpose of the protest. In this same vein, Regina asked Nigerians to show their concerns without resorting to violence.
