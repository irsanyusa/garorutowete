Ranking Our Favorite Taylor Swifts The Eras Tour Outfits

May 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images for TAS Rights Management

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Kicking off her most-awaited The Eras Tour in March, pop superstar Taylor Swift has been enrapturing Swiftie hearts with her three-hour music-packed show. Aside from her glorious vocals though, fans are also blown away by her ethereal costume changes.

Wearing more than 40 outfits amid tour, the pop star has definitely served and “mothered” with her exquisite visuals. We chose and ranked our favorite Taylor Swift The Eras Tour costumes. From bodysuits to ballgowns, here are where we think she looked best. 

Our Favorite Taylor Swift Outfits on The Eras Tour

10. Her Lilac Alberta Ferretti “Folklore” Dress

During her Folklore set in Glendale, Arizona, Swift wore a flowing lilac Alberta Ferretti gown to reminisce her 2020 era. Although purple is not the main color theme of the album, the dress’ flowy detail gave off a deluxe vibe. 

9. The Green Alberta Ferretti “Folklore” Dress

Among all of Swift’s Folklore dresses, her green flowy ensemble suited her the best. From its plunging neckline to leaves embroideries, this dress made Swift look like a forest fairy. 

8. The Silver Versace “Lover” Blazer

There’s no better way to perform “The Man” than in a glittery and shimmering blazer. For her performance in Las Vegas, Nevada, Swift sported a custom silver blazer matched with a pair of Christian Louboutin knee-high boots. 

7. The Midnight Blue Oscar de la Renta “Midnights” Bodysuit

Swift typically slips on a sparkling bodysuit during her “Midnights” set. Her original navy Oscar de la Renta one-piece suit is a particular favorite as it resembles her dark blue-sounding album. Swift wore this ensemble during her show in Nashville, Tennessee.

6. The Blue Nicole + Felicia “Speak Now” Ballgown

Sporting a blue ball gown by Nicole + Felicia, Swift looked ethereal when she performed at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles. From its halter top to floral designs, you’d think blue is the main color of Speak Now album.

5. The Roberto Cavalli “Reputation” Catsuit

During her Seattle show, Swift wore a bold one-legged jumpsuit. The Roberto Cavalli piece features an intricate red pattern of snakes from top to bottom. Its black overall color emphasized the singer sexy figure, putting more danger in the vibe of her Reputation set.

4. The Pink Zuhair Murad “Speak Now” Ballgown

Out of all the “Sparks Fly” singer’s Speak Now ball gowns, her pink-hued Zuhair Murad creation looked other-wordly. During her “Enchanted” set in East Rutherford, Swift undeniably looked like a real-life princess. The beauty of the ballgown was further emphasized as it popped against the stage’s purple lighting.

3. The Roberto Cavalli “Fearless” Dress

To recollect the nostalgia of “You Belong With Me” and “Love Story,” Swift’s flapper-inspired shimmery dress worked best on stage. She hyped the Glendale crowd with the said piece, matched with chic knee-high boots. 

2. The Zuhair Murad “Midnights” Bodysuit

Back in her Eras Tour show in Las Vegas, Swift wore a midnight blue bodysuit covered in blue crystals and beaded fringes. She added further detail into the ensemble by slipping on a matching blue garter. Indeed, Murad did a great job in putting a scalloped detailing on its top part.

1. The Pink and Blue Atelier Versace “Lover” Bodysuit

Wearing a custom Atelier Versace bodysuit with pink and blue jewels, Swift’s outfit resembled her Lover album artwork. This might arguably be the best outfit to open the show while singing “Miss Americana & the Heartbreak Prince.” When she wore this during her Las Vegas show, she added a flower necklace by Versace to elevate the look.
