These 10 Trendy Golf Brands Are Redefining Fairway Style

June 2024 · 7 minute read

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

Golf has had quite the glow-up in the past half-decade, all in thanks to our dear friend COVID. Because let’s face it, when everything else was shut down, hitting the links was a pretty great option for getting outdoors while keeping your distance. Furthermore, LIV Golf emerged to challenge the long-standing traditions of the PGA Tour, pushing boundaries not only in gameplay but also in the realm of fashion.

Since then, the game has grown as a more inclusive sport, in both style and culture, welcoming a diverse range of players who bring their own unique flair to the fairways.  

While big-name sportswear brands still hold influence, a fresh wave of brands has emerged, causing ripples with their distinct fusion of style, technology, and performance. From sleek newcomers challenging the status quo to heritage labels revitalizing their approach, here are a few brands that are reshaping style on the course.

Sunday Swagger coined the phrase “polos with personality,” and it couldn’t be more true. These eye-catching designs and vibrant colors offer unparalleled comfort and style, perfect for making a bold statement on and off the green. Plus, with matching patterns and colors available for both men and women, you can coordinate effortlessly for your next team scramble. 

Grayson Clothiers

Greyson Clothiers strikes a balance between traditional golf aesthetics and modern design. Their attention to detail is evident in every stitch and pattern, creating garmetns that are as visually striking as they are functional. Using premium materials like Pima cotton and innovative technical fabrics, Greyson ensures comfort, durability, and performance in every piece. While their offerings may come at a higher price point, they cater to individuals who value superior craftsmanship and distinctive style.

J. Lindeberg

Buy Now $85

J.Lindeberg stands out in the golf apparel industry by seamlessly blending contemporary fashion trends with functional golf clothing. Take, for example, the Lerato Women’s Polo which follows current fashion trends with a boxy, cropped silhouette. Its distinct chunky ribbed collar further enhances its stylish appeal, showcasing the brand’s commitment to combining fashion-forward elements with golf-specific functionality. 

Bad Birdie Golf

Buy Now $38

Bad Birdie Golf blends vibrant colors with quirky patterns, pushing the boundaries of traditional golf attire. From polos that pop to hats that scream “fore,” Bad Birdie Golf apparel brings a playful touch to your game. But don’t be fooled by the fun; their gear is crafted with high-quality materials to keep you comfortable and focused throughout your round.

lululemon

Lululemon’s ABC Pants are the ultimate choice for men who value style and performance. While they may appear to be a typical khaki pant, they’re so much more. With cutting-edge stretch, sweat-wicking tech, and mobility features, they seamlessly transition from the office to the golf course. Perfect for the style-forward guy seeking comfort and versatility.

Nike

Buy Now $150

The Air Jordan 1 Low G golf shoes are essential for golfers seeking the perfect balance of style and performance. Inspired by the timeless Air Jordan 1 silhouette, these bring street style to the greens. Crafted with premium leather and incorporating Nike’s revolutionary Air cushioning technology, they offer luxurious support and responsiveness with every step and swing.

Vuori

Buy Now $64

Vuori is a frontrunner in activewear clothing, catering not only to yoga and strength training enthusiasts but also to those hitting the links. The Pique Women’s Polo offers a blend of simplicity and trendiness with its cropped style. Paired with the Volley Skort, the ensemble effortlessly transitions from the pickleball court to the tee box. Vuori’s Aim Short for men are a favorite among golfers for not only their comfort, but also its moisture-wicking, durable design.  

Malbon x Adidas

Buy Now $156

Malbon x Adidas combines urban aesthetics with golf-specific performance with hooded pullover, creating a standout piece suitable for both on and off the course. The lightweight material and loose fit provide comfort and ease of movement, making it ideal for active days on the fairways and around town.  Malbon is most known for bringing urban style to the fairways, with unique collaborations including  Jimmy Choo, Curb Your Enthusiasm and more. 

G/Fore

Buy Now $150

G/Fore has gained a reputation for their exceptional quality and style. From striking color combinations to modern silhouettes, each pair is a statement piece that elevates your on-course look. 

Peter Millar

Buy Now $145

MLB and collegiate sports fans can seamlessly rep their favorite teams on the links with a range of premium performance apparel including polos, quarter-zips, and vests. Made from premium materials, Peter Millar’s apparel offers superior comfort, moisture-wicking properties, and enhanced breathability, ensuring you stay cool and focused throughout your round. Whether you’re teeing off or cheering on your team, Peter Millar’s MLB Golf Polos and performance wear are the perfect choice for combining sports passion with golfing excellence.

TravisMatthew

Buy Now $39.95

The subtle (yet obvious) humor among TravisMathew attire makes this brand a favorite among avid and casual golfers. With pieces like their “Golf Sucks” women’s tee and Between the Lines Golf Glove, the brand adds a playful touch to the challenging sport. Jokes aside, TravisMathew brings a relaxed California vibe to their designs, incorporating wrinkle-resistant performance fabrics – perfect for work and play.
